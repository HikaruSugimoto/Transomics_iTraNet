Differentially expressed mRNAs and associated transcription factors (TFs) and miRNAs. The associated TFs and miRNAs are estimated using the ChIP-Atlas (https://chip-atlas.org/enrichment_analysis) and miRTarBase (https://mirtarbase.cuhk.edu.cn/~miRTarBase/miRTarBase_2022/php/download.php) databases, respectively.
